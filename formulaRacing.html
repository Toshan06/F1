FORMULA MOTORSPORT

This is the official webpage of Formula 1

Formula 1, often abbreviated as F1, is the highest class of international single-seater auto racing sanctioned by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA). It’s known for being one of the most prestigious and technologically advanced forms of motorsport, featuring incredibly fast cars, cutting-edge technology, and global fanfare.

The first Formula one World Drivers' Champion was Giuseppe Farina in the 1950 Championship.

The current WDC Holder is Max Verstaappen in the 2023 season.
